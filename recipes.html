ADOBO RECIPE

Ingredients:

Names Quantity
Chicken Thighs 3kg
Oil 2tbsp
Soy Sauce 2/3 cup
Vinegar 1/3 cup
Garlic 2tbsp
Oyster sause 2tbsp
Light Brown Sugar 2tbsp
Bay Leaves 1 pack
Black Peppercorns 2tbsp

Steps:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a big pot.

  2. Add chicken thighs skin side down and sear on both sides.

  3. Add the garlic, oyster sauce, and soy sauce.

  4. Mix to coat the chicken.

  5. Add the vinegar, brown sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns.

  6. Bring the liquid to a boil and lower to a simmer.

  7. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour.

  8. Flip the chicken every 20 minutes, so each side gets an even amount of color.

  9. Serve chicken over a bed of rice.
